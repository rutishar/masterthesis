\chapter{Literature Screening Sheet}
\label{app:screening}

The following columns are used in the Scopus literature screening Excel file:

\begin{table}[htbp]
  \centering
  \caption{Literature screening sheet columns}
  \label{tab:screening_columns}
  \begin{tabular}{@{}lp{9cm}@{}}
    \toprule
    \textbf{Column} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \midrule
    Author(s)   & Author names \\
    Year        & Publication year \\
    Title       & Full paper title \\
    Source      & Journal / Conference \\
    Relevance   & Low / Medium / High — assessed against the four RQs \\
    Finding     & Key insight relevant to this research \\
    Category    & Which RQ or theme the paper primarily addresses \\
    Notes       & Additional remarks \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
\end{table}
